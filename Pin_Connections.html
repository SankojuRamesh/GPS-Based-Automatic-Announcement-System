Touch Controller — XPT2046
ESP32-S3 GPIO Signal / Define XPT2046 Pin Direction
GPIO 11 TFT_MOSI DIN  (Data In, shared with TFT) OUT
GPIO 12 TFT_SCLK CLK  (Clock, shared with TFT) OUT
GPIO 13 TOUCH_MISO DOUT  (Data Out) IN

3.3 V VCC VCC PWR
GND GND GND GND
SPI Frequencies (User_Setup.h):   TFT Write = 27 MHz  |  TFT Read = 20 MHz  |  Touch = 2.5 MHz
Shared SPI bus: GPIO 11 (MOSI) and GPIO 12 (SCK) are shared between TFT and Touch.
MISO line: GPIO 13 is used for both TFT read-back and touch data — CS lines ensure only one device is active at a time.

SD Card Module
SPI Mode — Arduino SD Library  |  Separate MISO line (GPIO 40)
ESP32-S3 GPIO Signal / Define SD Card Pin Direction
GPIO 11 MOSI DI / CMD  (Data In) OUT
GPIO 12 SCK CLK / SCLK  (Clock) OUT
GPIO 15 SD_CS CS / SS  (Chip Select, active LOW) OUT
GPIO 40 SD_MISO DO / DAT0  (Data Out — dedicated line) IN
3.3 V VCC VCC PWR
GND GND GND GND
Dual-MISO design: SD uses GPIO 40, Touch uses GPIO 13. Before every SD operation the firmware calls:
  SPI.end() → SPI.begin(SCK=12, MISO=40, MOSI=11, SS=15)
After SD is done it switches back to GPIO 13 for touch. SD CS (GPIO 15) is held HIGH during touch reads.

GPS Module
L89H or any NMEA module — ESP32-S3 HardwareSerial UART1 — TinyGPSPlus
ESP32-S3 GPIO Signal / Define GPS Module Pin Direction
GPIO 16 GPS_RX (UART1 RX) TX  (GPS module transmits here) IN
GPIO 17 GPS_TX (UART1 TX) RX  (GPS module receives here) OUT
3.3 V VCC VCC PWR
GND GND GND GND
UART1: Initialised with gpsSerial.begin(baud, SERIAL_8N1, GPS_RX=16, GPS_TX=17).
Baud rate: Standard NMEA = 9600 bps  |  L89H supports up to 115200 bps — match in firmware.
Logic level: Most GPS modules are 3.3 V. If yours is 5 V, use a level shifter on TX/RX lines.
4
I2S Audio Amplifier — MAX98357A
ESP-IDF Raw I2S Driver (I2S_NUM_0) — WAV playback from SD card
ESP32-S3 GPIO Signal / Define MAX98357A Pin Direction
GPIO 35 ANN_I2S_BCK_PIN BCLK  (Bit Clock) OUT
GPIO 36 ANN_I2S_WS_PIN LRC  (Word Select / LRCK) OUT
GPIO 37 ANN_I2S_DOUT_PIN DIN  (Serial Audio Data) OUT
3.3 V or Float SD_MODE SD  (Shutdown / Enable) CONFIG
Float GAIN GAIN  (Gain select) CONFIG
5 V VIN VIN  (3.0 – 5.5 V supply) PWR
GND GND GND GND
SD_MODE: 3.3 V or floating = amplifier enabled  |  GND = shutdown.
GAIN pin: Float = 9 dB  |  GND = 12 dB  |  3.3 V = 15 dB  |  100kΩ to GND = 6 dB.
Speaker: Connect to OUT+ / OUT− differential outputs (no capacitor needed).
Power: Controlled by relay (Module 5). Relay turns ON 150 ms before playback, OFF after audio ends.
5
Amplifier Power Relay
Switches 5 V supply to MAX98357A — power saving between announcements
ESP32-S3 GPIO Signal / Define Relay Module Pin Direction
GPIO 38 AMP_RELAY_PIN IN  (Control signal) OUT
5 V VCC VCC  (Relay coil supply) PWR
GND GND GND GND
5 V rail COM COM  (Common — relay input side) PWR
MAX98357A VIN NO NO  (Normally Open — to amp supply) SWITCHED
GPIO 38 HIGH → Relay ON → 5 V connected to MAX98357A VIN → Amplifier powered.
GPIO 38 LOW  → Relay OFF → 5 V disconnected from MAX98357A VIN → Amplifier off.
Relay coil (VCC/GND) is powered separately from the switched load. If using a transistor-driven relay board, ensure the board's logic input is compatible with 3.3 V from ESP32-S3.
6
Battery Monitor
3S LiPo Pack (9.0 – 12.6 V) via Resistor Voltage Divider → ESP32-S3 ADC
Connection Point Signal / Component Detail Type
Battery (+) VBAT 3S LiPo positive — 9.0 V (empty) to 12.6 V (full) SOURCE
R1 (top) 40 kΩ resistor From Battery(+) to GPIO 1 mid-point node PASSIVE
GPIO 1 BAT_ADC_PIN ADC mid-point — reads 1800 mV (empty) to 2520 mV (full) ANA IN
R2 (bottom) 10 kΩ resistor From GPIO 1 mid-point node to GND PASSIVE
Battery (−) / GND GND Common ground GND
Divider ratio: R2 ÷ (R1 + R2) = 10k ÷ 50k = 0.2
12.6 V (3 × 4.2 V, fully charged) → 2520 mV at GPIO 1   |   9.0 V (3 × 3.0 V, empty) → 1800 mV at GPIO 1
Firmware uses analogReadMilliVolts() (ESP32-S3 calibrated ADC) with a 32-sample sorted average, trimming the top and bottom 25% to reject noise.
